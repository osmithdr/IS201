Why Poland?

Jump to Photos

I am a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. As a member of this church, I moved across the world to do missionary service in Poland. As a missionary I spent my days helping people. We would meet with people by video chat or in person and share messages about Jesus Christ. Because I was living in a foreign land, I had to learn polish, learn how to get around the country, and learn how to connect with the people living there. If you would like to see some pictures of my time in Poland, skroll down!
